About

THG Services LLC is a software company headquartered in Roswell, Georgia. We design, build, and support our own products — handling everything from initial concept and engineering through customer onboarding and long-term support.

Our team focuses on the unglamorous-but-critical operational work that keeps small businesses running: making sure no lead goes cold, no customer message is missed, and no opportunity falls through the cracks.

We work directly with the businesses that use our software, and we ship product based on what they actually need.
